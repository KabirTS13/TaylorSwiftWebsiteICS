About Her

Taylor Swift is an American singer and lyricist who is a role model for all ages, persevering through various conflicts that would otherwise kill a lesser person. Taylor has a charitable, charismatic, and caring personality, being dedicated to her art and fans. This has led her to be one of the biggest pop stars of her generation and arguably the best. She has won many awards with her incredible music hits, and her newly released album continues to break various records.

Fandom - The Swifties

Taylor Swift's fan base is called "Swifties." Swifties are people from all around the world who are fans of Taylor Swift for her music and energetic and loving personality. They support her and try to make their queen's day better, as well as supporting her in difficult times. For example, Taylor was having a difficult time after the Kayne-West incident, getting bombarded with haters and Kayne West fans. After years of public shame, Taylor went in hiding and came out with Reputation, showing her changed personality. To support this, fans bought multiple copies and shared it with others.

Charity Work

Taylor Swift has become well-known for her charitable donations, lending a hand whenever she can. Her most recent donation was $15 000 to a 19-year old fan whose mother has been in a coma for the past three years (since 2021). Her first donation was made in October 2011; she donated $70 000 worth of books (6 000 books) to the Reading Public Library in Pennsylvania while doing her Speak Now tour. As well as donating $250 000 to Kesha with her case against Dr. Luke (which was not going in Kesha's favour), as she was nearing bankruptcy. Furthermore, Taylor Swift also gave the money made from her wildlife-themed “Wildest Dreams” video to the African Parks Foundation of America (APFA).
