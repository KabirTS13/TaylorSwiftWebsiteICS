How the Quiz Works

You will be tested on your knowledge of Taylor Swift, with questions ranging from her DOB, full name, albums, and pets. Based on the number of questions you get right, you will be classified as a “fan,” “hardcore fan,” “Swiftie,” “listens to some songs,” or “doesn't listen.” On the bottom of the page, there will also be links to other quizzes available. There is a timer of 10 minutes, so act quickly. Once the time runs out, you will get an alert with your score.
